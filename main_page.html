Project Summary

In today’s rapidly evolving digital environment, staying compliant with privacy laws like the EU GDPR is both essential and complex. The GDPR Scanner is an AI-powered, lightweight web tool that helps simplify privacy compliance for websites and apps.

It automatically analyzes your privacy policy, cookie usage, and consent mechanisms to check for alignment with regulations such as the EU GDPR and Google Consent Mode v2. Future support for UK GDPR, PIPEDA, and Law 25 (Canada) is planned.

Designed for startups, web developers, privacy consultants, and small eCommerce businesses, this tool offers a fast, transparent, and cost-effective alternative to expensive legal compliance audits.

How It Works: Core Functionalities

1. GDPR Compliance Scanner – Key Issues Checked

Upload your privacy policy. Our scanner automatically analyzes it for GDPR compliance across 14 real-world focus areas:

🔍 What We Check:

  • ✅ Consent – Is valid user consent properly collected and documented?
  • 👤 Data Protection Officer (DPO) – Does your policy mention a DPO and their responsibilities?
  • 📧 Email Marketing – Are consent and opt-out mechanisms in place for communications?
  • 🔒 Encryption – Are security measures (like encryption) disclosed clearly?
  • ⚖️ Fines / Penalties – Are users informed about GDPR rights and potential violations?
  • 🗂️ Personal Data – Is the scope of personal data collection clearly defined?
  • 🛡️ Privacy by Design – Are privacy principles embedded in your data handling process?
  • 📋 Privacy Impact Assessment (PIA) – Are high-risk processing activities evaluated?
  • ⚙️ Processing – Is data processing lawful, limited, and transparent?
  • 📑 Records of Processing Activities (ROPA) – Are internal record-keeping practices mentioned?
  • 🔎 Right of Access – Can users request access to their personal data?
  • 🧹 Right to be Forgotten – Can users request data deletion?
  • ℹ️ Right to be Informed – Are users properly informed at the time of data collection?
  • 🌍 Third Countries – Are data transfers outside the EU addressed properly?

Scoring Labels: ✅ Compliant, ⚠ Potentially Compliant, ❌ Requires Review

Output Formats: JSON, Markdown, HTML (PDF export coming soon!)
